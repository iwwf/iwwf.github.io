### How WBTC Works  
- **Minting Process**: When a user deposits BTC into a WBTC custodian (e.g., BitGo), an equivalent amount of WBTC is minted on Ethereum.  
- **Redemption**: Users can burn WBTC to reclaim their BTC at any time.
